doppelganger

doppelganger is an unfinished existential platformer metroidvania about being the evil clone of the hero.

it's developed entirely by me! that includes coding, game design, art, music, etc. the one exception to this is that many sounds are sourced from freesound.org because I'm really bad at at sound design and they have many good ones there.

[SPOILERS]
in doppelganger, you succeed at killing the hero almost instantly. now you're left to ponder: what's next? you embark on a journey of self-discovery, into an unfriendly world. you fight enemies, fight bosses, talk to people, do some platforming challenges, and collect items along this journey.
